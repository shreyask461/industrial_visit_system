About The App

Industrial visits can prove to be beneficial for both the student and faculty as certain concepts cannot be explained within the constraints of a class room. The schools and colleges from all over the city use various ways to plan and book an industrial visit.

Our application will provide a platform for the user where they will learn about the various visiting places like a catalogue from which they can chose an appropriate place to visit as per their requirements as well as provide a platform to book and plan visits.
